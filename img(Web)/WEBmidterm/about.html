About Me

Lyndan was born in Salt Lake City, Utah and has lived all over the west coast in Washington, Oregon, and California. After high school, She moved to Boulder, Colorado to study Marketing at the University of Colorado Boulder Leeds School of Business and minor in Technology, Arts, and Media. She plans to move to Boston, MA when she finishes her bachelors degree in May 2023.

During high school, Lyndan discovered her passion for graphic design. She has designed a number of graphics for various companies including Patagonia, San Diego Union High School District, and sold some of her designs to Kappa Kappa Gamma Boulder.

She has worked a number of jobs including a Marketing Internship at Wasserman, Assistant Dental Hygenist at Bob Randolph D.D.S, and currently works as a Sales Associate for HOTWORX Boulder.

Lyndan enjoys traveling, trying new restruants, skiing, visiting art museums, and spending quality time with family and friends.

After college, she plans to find a job at a marketing agency, working for a variety of clients and designing campaigns. Her studies of business administration and creative design will be the perfect background for her post-graduate career.
